# Product Roadmap - ScholarQuest (11+ Prep)

This roadmap outlines planned features to enhance the 11+ preparation experience, focusing on engagement, analytics, and comprehensive subject coverage.

## Core Learning Features

### 1. Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning
*   **Description:** Add dedicated modules for Verbal Reasoning (VR) and Non-Verbal Reasoning (NVR), which are critical components of GL and CEM exams.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   [x] **Verbal Reasoning:** Code-breaking, sequences, logical deduction, hidden words, and compound words generators.
    *   [ ] **Non-Verbal Reasoning:** Image-based questions for NVR (sequences, matrices, 3D rotation).

### 2. Timed Practice Mode (Completed)
*   **Description:** Allow users to set custom timers for practice sessions, not just mock exams, to build speed and accuracy.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   [x] Toggleable timer per question (60s default).
    *   [x] "Blitz" mode (answer as many as possible in 1 minute).

## Phase 3: Advanced Features & Adaptive Learning

### 3. AI-Driven Adaptive Difficulty & Personalization
*   **Description:** Move beyond static playlists to real-time difficulty adjustment (e.g., ELO rating system) inspired by platforms like Atom Learning.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   Dynamic difficulty scaling for Maths and Vocabulary.
    *   "Smart Tutor" algorithm that intervenes with easier questions after consecutive failures.
    *   **Digital Flashcards:** Spaced repetition system for vocabulary definitions and math formulas.

### 4. Exam-Board Specific Modes
*   **Description:** Tailor the experience to specific exam formats (GL Assessment, ISEB, CSSE).
*   **Implementation:**
    *   Toggleable exam modes that adjust timer logic and question types.
    *   **GL Mode:** Standard multiple choice.
    *   **ISEB Mode:** Adaptive online test simulation.
    *   **CSSE Mode:** Mixed standard and multiple choice with harder English sections.

### 5. Multimedia Learning
*   **Description:** Video walkthroughs for complex topics.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   **Video Explanations:** Short clips explaining the "how" behind Non-Verbal Reasoning and Advanced Maths questions.

## Phase 4: Community & Premium Content

### 6. Community Forum
*   **Description:** A moderated space for parents to discuss 11+ strategies and share resources.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   "Parent Connect" discussion board.
    *   Expert Q&A sessions.

### 7. Downloadable Resources
*   **Description:** Offline materials to supplement digital learning.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   Printable mock exams and answer sheets.
    *   Topic-specific worksheets (e.g., "The Ultimate Guide to Ratios").

## Engagement & Gamification

### 8. Gamification System (Partially Completed)
*   **Description:** Increase student motivation through rewards.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   [x] **Badges:** "Math Whiz", "Streak Master", "Early Bird".
    *   [x] **Boss Battles:** Every 5th streak triggers a high-difficulty challenge for bonus points.
    *   **Avatars:** Unlockable avatars based on points/levels.
    *   **Leaderboards:** Weekly/Monthly leaderboards (optional/anonymous).

## Interface & Accessibility

### 9. Focus Mode (Completed)
*   **Description:** A distraction-free UI for serious study sessions.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   [x] Toggleable "Focus Mode" in header.
    *   [x] Removes animations and switches to high-contrast theme.

## Analytics & Reporting

### 10. Detailed Analytics Dashboard & Auto-Marking
*   **Description:** Visual insights into progress with instant feedback.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   **Auto-Marking:** Instant grading of mock tests with detailed breakdown.
    *   [x] Line graphs showing score trends over time.
    *   Heatmaps for topic mastery (Green = Mastered, Red = Needs Work).
    *   Compare performance against average/target levels for specific schools.

### 11. Parent Dashboard & Reports
*   **Description:** A separate view or email report for parents to track their child's progress without intervening in the game.
*   **Implementation:**
    *   Weekly email summaries.
    *   "Problem Areas" alerts.

## Technical Enhancements
*   **PWA Support:** Make the app installable on tablets/phones.
*   **Offline Mode:** Cache questions for on-the-go practice.
